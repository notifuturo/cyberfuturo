Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-04-11 · Version: v0.1

This is a plain-language privacy policy for CyberFuturo. It is short because we collect very little.

What we collect

We collect exactly one thing from readers who interact with this site: the email address you enter into the subscribe form, plus the timestamp of that submission.

We do not collect names, IP addresses (beyond what any web server logs by default), browser fingerprints, demographic data, employer information, or anything else. The subscribe form has one field, and that field is the email address.

How we use it

Your email is used to send you CyberFuturo briefs and to honor unsubscribe requests. That is its only use.

What we do not do

  • We do not share your email with sponsors. Not in aggregate. Not by ICP segment. Not ever.
  • We do not sell, rent, or swap subscriber lists.
  • We do not run marketing-automation scoring against subscribers.
  • We do not track opens, clicks, or link activity beyond what is strictly necessary to identify and resolve delivery failures.
  • We do not attempt to re-identify subscribers across services.

Analytics

The site may use Cloudflare Web Analytics — a GDPR-compatible, cookie-less analytics product that aggregates anonymous page-view counts without any user-identifying information. At v0.1 this is disabled; when enabled, it will appear in a subsequent privacy-policy changelog entry.

We do not use Google Analytics, Segment, Mixpanel, Plausible, or any other third-party analytics product that ships a tracking pixel or a cookie.

Cookies

The site sets no cookies. The subscribe form is a plain HTML POST — no session cookies, no tracking cookies, no authentication cookies.

Third-party services

The subscribe form currently submits to formsubmit.co, which forwards the submission to the editor's email address. The form data that transits formsubmit.co is your email and nothing else. Once we migrate the mailing list to a newsletter platform (beehiiv is the current target), this section will update with the new data flow and a changelog entry.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which logs basic request metadata (IP, user-agent, URL) for the purpose of delivering the page and protecting against abuse. Cloudflare's privacy terms apply to that data; we do not receive identifiable request logs or use them for any analytical purpose.

Unsubscribing

Every email has a working unsubscribe link. Clicking it removes you from the list immediately with no confirmation step. If the unsubscribe link ever fails, reply to the email with "unsubscribe" in the subject and we will remove you manually.

Data retention

We retain subscriber email addresses for as long as you are subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your email is removed from the active sending list immediately. A suppression record (a one-way hash of the email) may be retained to prevent accidentally re-adding you if you resubscribe to a different channel.

Your rights

If you are a reader in a jurisdiction with a data-subject rights framework (EU GDPR, UK GDPR, California CPRA, etc.), you may request: access to your data (there is only the email and timestamp), correction, deletion, or export. To exercise any of these, reply to any CyberFuturo email or open an issue on the repo. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes

Changes to this policy are announced in the next brief with a changelog entry and the "Last updated" date above is revised. We do not make material changes without notifying subscribers. A material change is anything that broadens what we collect or how we use it.

Changelog

  • v0.1 — 2026-04-11 — Initial publication. Collection limited to email + timestamp via formsubmit.co; no analytics enabled; no cookies set.

This policy is written to be readable, not to cover every corner. If you find an ambiguity, ask, and we will clarify in the next version.
