How the future is arriving, by the numbers.

Public, reproducible indices on the pace of technology. No forecasts, no opinion — just the data, refreshed every month.

AI research velocity (arXiv, cs.AI + cs.LG)

How many new artificial intelligence and machine learning papers are submitted to arXiv each month. This doesn't measure quality, impact, or originality — just raw volume, as a thermometer of AI research activity.

Refreshed automatically on the 2nd of every month, straight from the arXiv API. Full methodology  ·  Raw data (CSV)  ·  CC-BY-4.0 license

What this is

CyberFuturo Index is a growing collection of public, reproducible indicators on how fast technology is moving. Each index documents its own source, methodology, and limitations — and none of them forecast. Just honest measurement, refreshed automatically.

This is the first one. More are coming.
